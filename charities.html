Resala

Help poor, needy people, and children by donating

Welcome

“Resala” originated on June 25, 1999 as a student activity in the Faculty of Engineering at Cairo University by a group of students who studied the engineering ethics course in the Department of Communications Engineering at the faculty by Dr. Sherif Abdel Azim.Risalah has more than 60 branches spread throughout Egypt, and more than 200 thousand volunteers volunteer each year, serving millions of Egyptians in about 30 volunteer.

activities

activities, including caring for orphans, serving the elderly, sheltering street children, serving the blind, deaf, and people with special needs, and classes Free piety, illiteracy eradication, used clothing exhibitions, blood donation, computer education, languages, human development, the Noble Qur’an, aid for the needy, charitable caravans for poor villages, medical caravans, addiction treatment, feeding the needy, making productive projects for the poor, building mosques and schools Fulfilling the wishes of cancer children, raising moral awareness for youth, cleaning streets and parks, spreading happiness among people, and being kind to animals.
